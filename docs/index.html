Quick Start Guide

1

Grant Microphone Access

When you first open CentiGrade, you'll be asked to allow microphone access. This is required for the app to hear your instrument. If you accidentally denied access, you can enable it in Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Microphone.

2

Select Your Tuning

Tap the tuning preset button in the top-left corner to choose your instrument's tuning. Options include Chromatic (all notes), Guitar Standard, Bass Standard, and more. You can also create custom tunings.

3

Play a Note

Play a single note on your instrument. The app will display the detected note name, octave, and frequency. The cents meter shows how far you are from perfect pitch.

4

Tune to Perfection

Adjust your instrument until the cents meter shows 0 (or close to it). When you're within ¬±5 cents, the display will glow green to indicate you're in tune!

üí° Pro Tips

  • Quiet Environment: For best results, tune in a quiet space to minimize background noise.
  • Play Clearly: Play notes cleanly and let them ring out. Avoid playing multiple strings at once.
  • Adjust Sensitivity: If the tuner is too jumpy or not responsive enough, adjust the sensitivity in Settings.
  • Visual Modes: Try the rolling trace mode to see your pitch stability over time.
